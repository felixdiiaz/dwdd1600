Important Tour Information

Group & Intitutional Discounts:

For larger groups (e.g., school classes, university groups), tour packages tend to be more affordable on a per-person basis. The cost can drop by as much as 10-30% depending on the size of the group and the specifics of the package.

Budget-Friendly Options:

UVU Airlines may also offer flexible payment plans or budget-conscious packages. These could include:

Group Discounts: For schools or universities booking multiple packages. Early-Bird Pricing: For bookings made several months in advance. Customizable Add-Ons: To control costs by choosing only the essentials or opting for more affordable lodging/transportation.
